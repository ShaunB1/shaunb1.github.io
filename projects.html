Recipe & Ingredient Management App (RIMA)

Used Java, Swing, MySQL

The Recipe and Ingredient Management App (RIMA) facilitates a quick and convenient way to create or follow recipes. RIMA will come with preloaded recipes and the users can additionally add their recipes to the database. RIMA combines the numerous functionalities of similar apps, while also improving upon these features according to user feedback.
